About Me

Full Stack Developer

I'm an IT professional turned full stack developer who is passionate about making the world a better place through code and meaningful products. As a result of being in a field that is constantly changing and requires continuous learning, I thrive in ambiguity, and can learn new languages and frameworks quickly. I value collaboration and the beauty of building something greater than the sum of its parts.

I have previously worked at companies like Apple, Foursquare, JP Morgan Chase, and IBM as an Analyst/Associate. In 2011 I graduated with Honors from Syracuse University obtaining a dual Bachelor of Science in Finance and Information Technology.
